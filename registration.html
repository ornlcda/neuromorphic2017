Registration and Hotel Information

The conference venue and hotel is the Crowne Plaza Downtown Knoxville University. We have reserved a block of rooms at the hotel for $96 per night plus taxes and fees. Please click here to make a reservation in the room block or call the hotel at 865-522-2600, Monday – Friday, 8:30 AM – 5:30 PM EST, and ask to speak with Jamie Julius, the Reservations Manager, for special requests for early arrivals or departures later than the cut-off date. The deadline for reserving a room at the Crowne Plaza at the conference rate is June 16, 2017.

A short walk from the conference venue will take you to Knoxville's World's Fair Park, Market Square, and the Old City.
